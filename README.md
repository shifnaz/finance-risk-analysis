# Finance & Risk Analysis: Credit & Market Risk Modeling (2021)

## 📌 Project Overview
This project provides a comprehensive analysis of financial risk, split into two domains: Credit Risk (predicting corporate default) and Market Risk (analyzing stock volatility and returns).

## 📊 Key Problems Solved
1. **Corporate Credit Risk Modeling:**
   - Analyzed financial health data for **3,500+ Indian companies** using 24 key financial ratios.
   - Built a **Logistic Regression** model to predict the probability of default.
   - **Result:** The model successfully outperformed the traditional **Altman Z-Score**, providing a more accurate classification of "At-Risk" companies.
2. **Market Risk & Portfolio Analysis:**
   - Evaluated stock price trends for major firms (Infosys, Axis Bank, etc.).
   - Calculated daily returns and standard deviation to measure **Risk vs. Reward**.
   - Identified optimal risk-adjusted stocks using mean-variance plots.

## 🛠️ Technical Skills Applied
* **Predictive Modeling:** Logistic Regression for binary classification.
* **Financial Metrics:** Net Worth analysis, Capital Employed, and Working Capital ratios.
* **Market Analytics:** Volatility calculation (Standard Deviation), Trend Analysis, and Risk-Return profiling.
* **Data Cleaning:** Extensive pre-processing of messy financial datasets and handling of missing values.
